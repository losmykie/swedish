Numbers — Cardinal & Ordinal

Cardinals = how many. Ordinals = which position (first, second…). Ordinals are used for dates — e.g. den tredje maj (3rd of May).

Date example
Date tip: Swedish dates use ordinal + month — den femte juni = 5th of June. 1st & 2nd are irregular (första, andra). From 3rd onward add -e to the cardinal (tredje, fjärde, femte…). 11th (elfte) and 12th (tolfte) are also irregular.
